Decentralized Skill Passport

A blockchain-based application that stores and verifies user skills as NFTs. This system ensures tamper-proof validation of achievements and allows users to securely showcase their skills. Integrated with MetaMask, it enables users to mint and manage their skill records on the blockchain.

Tech Used: Blockchain, Smart Contracts, MetaMask, Web3
