Shatranj ka Dhisşaşa – Chess Engine

A custom-built chess engine developed in C++ using the Minimax algorithm with Alpha-Beta pruning. The engine evaluates board positions, generates legal moves, and makes optimal decisions based on search depth and evaluation heuristics.

Tech Used: C++, Minimax Algorithm, Alpha-Beta Pruning
