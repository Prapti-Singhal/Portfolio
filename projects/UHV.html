Intergenerational Interaction Study – Inclusive Design for Elderly Users

A human-centered research project focused on understanding interactions between younger and elderly generations. The study involved interviews, surveys, and observations to analyze communication gaps and social well-being among seniors.

The project highlights inclusive design in technology and how meaningful interaction can bridge generational gaps.

Methods Used: User Research, Interviews, Surveys, Data Analysis, Human-Centered Design
