Throat Chakra: Voice of Truth

A visual representation of the Throat Chakra (Vishuddha), symbolizing communication, self-expression, and truth. The use of cool blue tones and clean layout reflects clarity, openness, and authentic voice.

Third Eye Chakra: Seat of Intuition

A visual representation of the Third Eye Chakra (Ajna), symbolizing intuition, clarity, and inner wisdom. The use of indigo tones and structured composition reflects perception, awareness, and deeper understanding.

Crown Chakra: Gateway to Higher Consciousness

A visual representation of the Crown Chakra (Sahasrara), symbolizing spiritual connection, enlightenment, and higher consciousness. The use of violet tones and soft gradients reflects transcendence, awareness, and inner peace.
